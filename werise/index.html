Find a job

  • You are assumed to be AS skilled as everyone else.
  • Your code is assumed to be as good.
  • You are assumed to be correct.
  • You are listened to and not interupted.
  • The same language is used for everyone
  • Merit, not potential, are the bench marks.
  • A culture in which people stand up when others are silenced.

Successful Shops

  • Directives (instead of constructive criticism)
  • Improv principles
  • Everyone is onboarded
  • Everyone is included
  • Processes are formalized
